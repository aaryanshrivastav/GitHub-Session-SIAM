Vision 2047: Introduction

The All Indian Football Federation (AIFF) released its "Vision 2047", a roadmap for Indian football, covering various essential aspects of the game, including refereeing education, coaching education and more. The AIFF has structured this roadmap into multiple phases. There are six phases where each except for the last phase lasting a period of 4 years while the last phase is of 5 years which means currently, we are in the first phase, 2023 to 2026. All the phases are:

  • 2023-2026
  • 2027-2030
  • 2031-2034
  • 2035-2038
  • 2039-2042
  • 2043-2047

"Vision 2047" focuses on 11 different aspects of the footballing world, ranging from the clubs to the grassroots player development, from the governance to the education of the coaches and referees. This shift in priorities signifies that the AIFF is finally giving the sport the attention it deserves. The 11 aspects are:

  1. Governance
  2. Infrastructure
  3. Digital Transformation
  4. Refereeing
  5. Clubs
  6. Grassroots
  7. Talent Identification and Development
  8. Coaching
  9. Competitions
  10. National Teams
  11. Marketing and Commercialization

In the coming days, we will look at each of the aspects one by one under the first phase of 2023-26.
